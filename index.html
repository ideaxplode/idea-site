How do we build?
Technology has evolved. So have we.
Welcome to the modern software development paradigm

No-Code

No-code tools have changed the game for software development. From design to deployment, everything comes out of the box in a single platform. Mature no-code tools enable building even complex applications.
Traditional development is powerful — but often expensive and slow. No-code tools offer full-stack, all-in-one-platform experience — no need for meticulous technical choices on stack and architecture.
Mature platforms like Bubble are known for the huge number of successful & scalable apps that are already out in the wild. When you want to reliably build your idea, but with less resources, no-code tools can be a real blessing.
Bubble, WebFlow, Airtable, Glide, Zapier, n8n

Low-Code

When we try to build custom experiences using no-code tools and hit platform's limits, having coding expertise will keep us flying. Low-code grants unlimited extensibility of the no-code platform.
Think of low-code as precision engineering. Low-code allows us to add nuanced behavior, custom components, or refined UI elements.
It’s how we fine-tune your app exactly to realize your vision  — without losing the rapid pace of no-code development. Low-code is where productivity and control come together seamlessly.
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Bubble Plugins

AI-Code

AI-Code is not "vibe coding". Vibes simply cannot produce production-grade applications. Through a systematic, AI-native methodology, we make sure your software is secure, stable and scalable.
Generative AI can be like a PhD graduate at one moment and behave like a high school student at another. The hallucinating nature of LLMs sometimes makes regressive changes to code.
Our engineering process framework minimizes hallucinations and transforms the non-deterministic outputs of LLMs into deterministic, measurable outcomes. We call it AI Driven Development (AIDD).

Our human-in-the-loop workflow averts ambiguous code areas and reduces bugs. Rigorous (human & AI) testing makes sure that the app is performing as intended.

Nevertheless, with AI copilots, coding has become more strategic and less mechanical, enhancing productivity. We watch the developments in this space very closely to leverage cutting-edge coding agents and tools to develop software with clean, efficient code.
Claude Code, Cursor.ai, v0, Windsurf, Next.js, Supabase, Node.js, Express.js

AI-Build

You describe what you want, and the AI builder generates an app. Sounds simple. But real-world applications bring unexpected challenges: bugs, security gaps, or ambiguous behavior.
AI builders offer rapid prototyping ? but maintaining and scaling these apps to production-level quality requires deeper expertise. It usually is the difference between building toys vs tools.
AI builders usually produce "standard", boilerplate UI and functionality (because that's what they're trained on) that lacks creativity and is less exciting. A creative (or human) touch could be the differentiator that separates your product from a slew of others.

Our prompt engineering and context engineering skills enable us to have control and shape the outcome of these AI builders. If needed, we can export code from the AI builders and edit the code iteratively using coding agents — for even better control and customization.

Generative AI code can inadvertently introduce subtle bugs, security vulnerabilities, or unclear logic. We proactively address these issues by carefully curating prompts, refining AI-generated outputs, and rigorously testing for quality and security. By blending Agile methods with disciplined AI practices, we ensure your app is fit for the wild west.
Bubble AI, Replit, Lovable.dev, Bolt.new
